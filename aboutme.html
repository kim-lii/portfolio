Moar about me!!

My name is Kim Li and I am 15 years old. I go to Gunn High School in Palo Alto, and am a rising Junior. I spent the first part of summer at a three-week ballet intensive, where I trained with many professors and other teachers from various academies around the nation. I then took a week break with my family in Los Cabos, where we spent time relaxing by the beach and tasting various foods. Back in California, I began volunteering at an adult activity center that offers classes such as ceramics, yoga, zumba, et cetera. I also started studying for my SATs which I will soon be taking. I got invited to Girls Who Code at Amazon in Sunnyvale, and now I am studying at this seven-week program, learning many languages such as Python, HTML, and JavaScript.

A huge part of my life is at my dance studio. I'm no Joffrey material, but it is a hobby that I love and hope to continue throughout my life. I typically spend around fifteen hours at the studio, taking various classes such as ballet, pointe, jazz, modern, lyrical, contemporary, and those are just to name a few! Next spring, I will be traveling with my ballet company to compete at a national competition. Outside of the studio, I also joined my school dance team, Gunn Titanettes, which placed first at a national competition. Go Gunn Titans!
